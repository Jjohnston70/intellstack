## About This Analysis

### Methodology

This gap report was produced using the World Model Mapper framework, which analyzes business processes through five dimensions:

1. **State Discovery** — What information is tracked vs. missing
2. **Action Mapping** — What decisions are enabled and their latency
3. **Transition Modeling** — What predictions are made and their reliability
4. **Feedback Analysis** — Whether outcomes validate predictions
5. **Domain Physics** — Hard constraints that must be respected

### Shadow vs. Reality

A key concept in this analysis is the distinction between **shadow data** (what someone reported happened) and **reality data** (direct measurement of what actually happened). Most business systems over-rely on shadows, which can diverge significantly from reality.
