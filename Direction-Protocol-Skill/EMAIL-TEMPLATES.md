### Template 1: Post-IDENTIFY Follow-Up

**When to Use:** Within 24 hours of qualified IDENTIFY call
**Purpose:** Confirm ASSESS scheduling, build momentum

---

**Subject:** Next Step: Discovery Call - [Company Name]

---

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for taking the time to talk today. Based on our conversation about [main pain point they mentioned], I'd like to dig deeper into what's happening in your operation.

**Next Step:** A 30-minute discovery call where I'll ask more detailed questions about your workflows, data, and decision points. This helps me understand if and how we can help.

**Scheduled:** [Day, Month DD] at [X:XX pm MT]

I'll send a calendar invite shortly.

**What to Expect:**
- We'll talk about your typical day/week
- I'll ask where information lives and how decisions get made
- We'll discuss what "fixed" would look like for you

No preparation needed — just come ready to talk about how things actually work in your business.

Talk soon.

[Optional - choose one that fits:]

Best of luck with [their business/project].

### Template 4: Post-ASSESS Next Steps

**When to Use:** After ASSESS call when scheduling MAP session
**Purpose:** Confirm MAP scheduling, explain what happens next

---

**Subject:** MAP Session Scheduled - [Company Name]

---

Hi [First Name],

Great conversation today. Based on what you shared about [key pain point], I think there's a real opportunity to fix some things in your operation.

**Next Step:** A 90-minute MAP session where we go deep into your workflows, data sources, and decision points. This is the diagnostic that lets me scope out exactly what you need.

**Scheduled:** [Day, Month DD] at [X:XX am/pm MT]
**Duration:** 90 minutes (sometimes runs to 2 hours)
**Format:** Video call — [link]

**What to Prepare:**
- Be ready to walk me through your main workflows step by step
- Know where your key data lives (spreadsheets, apps, etc.)
- Have examples of things that have gone wrong recently
- If possible, have access to your systems so we can look together

This is diagnostic, not sales. I'm trying to understand your operation well enough to tell you exactly what needs to change and what it would cost. If it turns out we're not a fit, I'll tell you.

After the MAP session, I'll put together findings and pricing. Then we'll have a shorter CHART call where I present what I found and what I recommend.

Questions before our MAP session? Just reply here.

[Signature]
