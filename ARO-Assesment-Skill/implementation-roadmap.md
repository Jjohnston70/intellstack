## Decision Points

### After Quick Wins (Day 30)

**Review Questions:**
- Did quick wins deliver expected improvement?
- Are there obstacles to Phase 1 we didn't anticipate?
- Has anything changed in business priorities?

**Options:**
1. Proceed to Phase 1 as planned
2. Adjust Phase 1 scope based on learnings
3. Pause and reassess

### After Phase 1 (Day 90)

**Review Questions:**
- Is the foundation solid enough for Phase 2?
- What's the measured improvement so far?
- Is Phase 2 scope still appropriate?

**Options:**
1. Proceed to Phase 2 as planned
2. Adjust Phase 2 based on results
3. Move to maintenance mode (good enough for now)
4. Add additional scope to Phase 2
