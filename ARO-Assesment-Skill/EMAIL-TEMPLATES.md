- **Context Graph** — your business's "AI instruction manual"

**Timeline:** [5/7/10] days from kickoff to deliverables

**First Step:** Complete this intake questionnaire: [LINK]

It takes about 30-45 minutes. Once I have your responses, we'll schedule the discovery call.

If you proceed with implementation after the assessment, we credit $500 toward the project.

Questions? Just reply to this email.
